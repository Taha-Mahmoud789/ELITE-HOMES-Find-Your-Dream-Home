Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about buying, selling, and investing in real estate

You're ready to buy a home when you have stable income, a good credit score (typically 620+), a down payment saved (usually 10-20%), and you plan to stay in the area for at least 3-5 years. Our team can help assess your financial readiness.

Spring and early summer (March through June) are traditionally the best times to sell. However, market conditions, local factors, and your personal timeline are more important than the season.

Your offer should be based on comparable sales in the area, the home's condition, and current market conditions. Our agents will provide a detailed comparative market analysis (CMA) to help you make a competitive offer.

Closing costs typically range from 2-5% of the home's purchase price and include fees for loan origination, appraisal, title insurance, attorney fees, and taxes. Both buyers and sellers have closing costs.

Yes, absolutely! A professional home inspection typically costs $300-600 but can save you thousands by identifying potential issues. We recommend inspections for all property types.

For buyers, the process typically takes 30-45 days from offer acceptance to closing. For sellers, our properties typically sell within 28 days.
